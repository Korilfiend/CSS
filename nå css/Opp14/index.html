Hornet

Hornet is a skilled warrior and daughter of the Pale King in Hollow Knight.
She guards Hallownest and challenges the player with her needle and thread.

The Knight

The Knight is the silent protagonist of Hollow Knight, a small,
mysterious being on a journey through the fallen kingdom of Hallownest.
hey wield a nail as a weapon and seek to uncover the truth behind the
kingdom’s collapse.

Quirrel

Quirrel is a curious, wandering scholar in Hollow Knight.
He travels through Hallownest, studying its mysteries and offering insights
to the player. Friendly and knowledgeable, he has a deep interest
in the kingdom’s history.
